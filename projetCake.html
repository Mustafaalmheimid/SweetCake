About US

we are Sweet,a sweet shop based in Beirut, has been created in 2013 from chef Mohammad after his long term experience with sweets. All of our products are made from nature using family recipes with the only highest quality ingredients. We bake and sell fresh daily to ensure only the best products are sold to our consumers. We are so excited to share with you our sweets and cakes made with care and prepared with love. Our sweets are all you might need to make every day sweet and special as you are ! Meet Our Team

Our chefs' love for sweets began at a young age, and they have since honed their skills through pastry school, mastering the art of creating the perfect confections. They are not only talented in blending flavors, but also passionate about baking, bringing a creative and unique twist to their desserts. Their dedication and hard work have made them true experts in the field, and their sweets are sure to leave a lasting impression on anyone who tries them.
